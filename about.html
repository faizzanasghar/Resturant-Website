About Delizia Craft Pizza

At Delizia Craft Pizza, we believe that pizza is more than just food; it's an experience. Founded in 2024, our mission has always been to serve the freshest, most delicious pizzas made with high-quality ingredients.

Our Story

Muhammad Faizan Asghar, the founder of Delizia Craft Pizza and student of Artificial Intelligence at Fast National University, created Delizia Craft blending traditional methods with modern flavors.

Our Values

  • Quality Ingredients: We source our ingredients from local farms and trusted suppliers to ensure freshness and flavor in every bite.
  • Community Focus: We believe in giving back to the community that supports us. We regularly participate in local events and charities.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Our customers are our top priority. We strive to provide exceptional service and a welcoming atmosphere.

Join Us

We invite you to visit us at our location or order online to experience the deliciousness of our craft pizzas. Thank you for being a part of the Delizia Craft Pizza family!
